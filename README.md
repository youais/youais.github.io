# Yiwei Chai

Hello! I’m Yiwei Chai, a PhD student at Johns Hopkins University. I study exoplanets and their systems using a variety of observational methods.

My current projects involve using JWST observations to characterise sub-Neptune atmospheres, and developing end-to-end simulations to predict yields for transiting exoplanet atmosphere science with the upcoming Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope.

Previously, I have worked on investigating the dynamics and spectral composition of a debris disk—-brown dwarf system using direct imaging techniques, and figuring out how to quantify model comparison for exoplanet atmospheres through Bayesian evidence calibration.
